Veera is a troubled, obsessive photographer determined to prove his worth in a world that has long dismissed him.

Driven by ambition and haunted by a painful past, he ventures deep into Mathikettan Solai — a mysterious reserve forest — to capture a rare and elusive natural phenomenon that could change his life forever.

But the deeper he goes, the more the forest begins to photograph him back.
